# Screen 03 — Dashboard / Today’s Summary

## Goal
Show the user a concise AI-generated summary of today and a quick snapshot of emotional and productivity status.

## Required layout
- Page title: “Dashboard”
- Main section title: “Today’s Summary”
- Large highlight card with:
  - text: “You felt Good 😊”
  - a supportive summary sentence such as:
    “You had a productive day with positive interactions.”
  - glowing decorative orb or soft circular illustration on one side
- “Quick Stats” section with three compact cards:
  - Mood — Good — avg. today
  - Productivity — 78% — high
  - Stress — Medium — manageable
- “AI Insight” card with a personalized observation:
  - “You tend to feel more positive on days when you exercise. Keep it up! 💪”
- Centered button below insights:
  - “View All Insights”
- Bottom nav with Home active.

## Visual style
- Dark polished dashboard design.
- Purple and teal accents.
- Card hierarchy must be very clear.
- Use subtle charts/graphics only where helpful.

## Important details
- This screen should feel like the app is already “thinking” for the user.
- The summary card must be the strongest visual element.
- The quick stats must be compact and easy to scan.
