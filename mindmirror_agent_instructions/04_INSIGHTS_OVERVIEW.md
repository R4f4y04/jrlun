# Screen 04 — Insights Overview

## Goal
Create an overview of the user’s mental and behavioral trends in a way that feels analytical but still friendly.

## Required layout
- Page title: “Insights”
- Top tabs:
  - Overview
  - Trends
  - Patterns
- Overview must be selected.
- Main card titled: “Your Mind at a Glance”
- Subtitle: “This Week”
- Donut chart or ring chart in the card.
- Smile emoji or face icon in the center of the chart.
- Legend next to the chart with mood categories and percentages:
  - Good
  - Okay
  - Bad
- “Top Takeaway” card below with text such as:
  - “Your mood improves significantly when you exercise and get good sleep.”
- “Explore Deeper” section with three feature tiles:
  - Mood Trends
  - Habit Impact
  - Triggers
- Bottom nav with Insights active.

## Visual style
- Dark, premium analytics feel.
- Clean charts.
- Soft pastel data colors that still fit the dark UI.
- Rounded tiles with clear labels and icons.

## Important details
- The page should suggest deeper intelligence beyond basic journaling.
- The takeaway card must be highly readable and direct.
- The deeper exploration tiles should look tappable and useful.
