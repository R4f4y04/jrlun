# Screen 06 — Journal Detail / Entry View

## Goal
Show a single journal entry in detail, with AI analysis below it.

## Required layout
- Top bar with:
  - back arrow
  - date/time: “24 May 2024, 9:30 PM”
  - edit pencil icon
- Main journal text card with a full entry such as:
  - “Had a productive day. Finished my project and got great feedback from my team. Went to the gym in the evening and felt really good. Talked to a friend which lifted my mood. Grateful for today. 😊”
- Tag chips below the entry:
  - Productive
  - Exercise
  - Social
  - plus one add tag chip
- AI Summary card:
  - “You had a productive and positive day. Exercise and social interaction contributed to your good mood.”
- Detected Emotion row:
  - “Happy”
  - small icon
  - arrow or chevron
- Next Step Suggestion card:
  - “Keep maintaining this balance. Consider planning tomorrow early to stay ahead!”
- Bottom nav may remain visible if desired, but the main focus should be the entry content and AI analysis.

## Visual style
- Elegant dark reading view.
- Large readable text.
- Softly glowing analysis cards.
- Consistent purple accents.

## Important details
- The journal text must feel authentic and complete.
- The AI summary should be direct and insightful.
- The suggestion must feel supportive, not judgmental.
