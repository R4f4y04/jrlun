# Screen 08 — Patterns

## Goal
Show recurring behavioral patterns discovered by the AI.

## Required layout
- Page title: “Patterns”
- Main card titled: “Your Top Patterns”
- List of pattern insights with icons, such as:
  - “You sleep less than 6 hours on days you feel bad”
  - “You feel better on days you exercise”
  - “Your mood drops on Sundays”
  - “You are more productive in the mornings”
- Each pattern should appear as a rounded row or card.
- Bottom motivational card with a brain illustration and message:
  - “Patterns help you understand yourself better. Awareness is the first step towards change.”
- Bottom nav can show Insights active.

## Visual style
- Dark, reflective, intelligent.
- Card-based layout.
- Simple icons with emotional meaning.
- Premium glow effect without overdoing it.

## Important details
- Patterns should feel meaningful and personal.
- Use concise but strong wording.
- The motivational card should be visually distinct.
