# Screen 09 — AI Coach Chat

## Goal
Create a supportive conversational AI helper that guides the user in a calm and practical way.

## Required layout
- Page title: “AI Coach”
- Plus icon or action icon in the top right.
- Chat conversation layout with AI messages on the left and user messages on the right.
- First AI message:
  - “Hi Amaan! I’m here to help you understand yourself better. How are you feeling right now?”
- User reply bubble:
  - “A bit overwhelmed”
- AI response:
  - “I understand. You’ve had a lot on your plate this week. Would you like me to help you break things down?”
- Two choice buttons:
  - “Yes, please”
  - “Not right now”
- Bottom text input:
  - placeholder: “Type your message...”
- Send button in a circular style.

## Visual style
- Calm chat experience.
- Dark background.
- Soft avatars for AI.
- Rounded bubbles with purple accent for the user.
- Keep the tone warm and reassuring.

## Important details
- This should feel helpful, not clinical.
- The AI must sound emotionally aware.
- The layout should be simple and comfortable to use.
