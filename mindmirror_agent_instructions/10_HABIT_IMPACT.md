# Screen 10 — Habit Impact

## Goal
Explain how different habits affect the user’s mood, productivity, and energy.

## Required layout
- Page title: “Habit Impact”
- Filter dropdown or pill: “This Month”
- Vertical list of habit impact cards:
  - Exercise — Positive impact — “+40% better mood”
  - Good Sleep — Positive impact — “+35% more productive”
  - Social Interaction — Positive impact — “+30% happier”
  - Junk Food — Negative impact — “-20% energy”
  - Late Night Scroll — Negative impact — “-25% next day mood”
- Each item should have:
  - a small icon
  - habit name
  - impact label
  - short explanation or percentage
- Use color coding for positive vs negative outcomes.

## Visual style
- Dark analytics screen.
- Very clear hierarchy.
- Rounded cards with soft glow.
- Strong visual distinction between helpful and harmful habits.

## Important details
- The screen should make the relationship between habits and mood instantly understandable.
- Keep the content concise but complete.
