# Screen 11 — Weekly Report

## Goal
Summarize the user’s week in a visually satisfying and encouraging way.

## Required layout
- Page title: “Weekly Report”
- Date range below it:
  - “13 May - 19 May”
- Main overall mood card:
  - “Overall Mood”
  - “Good”
  - a smiling face ring chart or circular indicator
  - summary text:
    “You had more good days than bad days this week.”
- Highlights section with three star-based bullets:
  - “Most productive on Friday”
  - “Best mood after exercise”
  - “Stress was high on Tuesday”
- Final motivational card at the bottom with a mountain illustration and message:
  - “Small steps every day lead to big changes. Keep going! 🌱”

## Visual style
- Premium weekly summary layout.
- Very clean and motivational.
- Strong contrast and card separation.
- Purple gradient accents, but gentle and elegant.

## Important details
- This screen should feel like a reward or reflection checkpoint.
- The overall mood card should be the focal point.
- Highlights must be easy to scan quickly.
